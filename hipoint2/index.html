Hi-Point Services (I) Pvt. Ltd. is a professionally managed engineering company engaged in the design, manufacturing, supply, erection, commissioning, and after-sales service of specialized systems and equipment for the marine, defence, and petrochemical sectors.

With decades of industry experience, we provide integrated engineering solutions in the areas of sanitation systems, meteorological systems, and gas detection solutions. Our expertise covers the complete project lifecycle—from concept and engineering design to manufacturing, installation, commissioning, and long-term maintenance support.

Our product portfolio is supported by advanced technologies and strategic collaborations with reputed international principals, enabling us to deliver reliable and efficient systems that meet global quality and performance standards.

Operating under ISO 9001:2015 certified processes, we follow structured engineering, manufacturing, and project management practices to ensure consistent quality, timely execution, and dependable service delivery. Over the years, we have successfully executed projects across the Indian subcontinent, earning the trust of clients, consultants, and project authorities.

Through continuous improvement, technical capability, and strong project execution expertise, Hi-Point Services (I) Pvt. Ltd. has established itself as a trusted partner for sanitation systems, meteorological instrumentation, and gas detection systems, while providing comprehensive lifecycle support to our customers.
