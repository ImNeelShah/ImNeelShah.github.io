Our History

Hi-Point Services (I) Pvt. Ltd. has grown steadily to become a recognized engineering solutions provider for the marine, defence, petrochemical, power, and industrial sectors in India. From its early years, the company established a strong presence in the marine industry by providing specialized equipment and technical services to ports, shipyards, and naval establishments across the country. Through consistent project execution and reliable service support, Hi-Point Services (I) Pvt. Ltd. gradually expanded its operations to cover most of the major and minor ports in India, including remote locations such as Port Blair.

Over time, the company diversified its activities and strengthened its technical capabilities to support a wider range of engineering systems and industrial applications. Hi-Point Services (I) Pvt. Ltd. initially built its reputation in the service sector by providing installation, commissioning, maintenance, and overhaul services for specialized marine and industrial equipment. As the organization grew, it expanded into turnkey project execution and system integration, undertaking projects that required design, engineering, supply, erection, commissioning, and lifecycle support.

With continuous technological advancement and collaboration with international technology partners, Hi-Point Services (I) Pvt. Ltd. broadened its product and system portfolio. The company progressively expanded its scope to include solutions such as sewage treatment plants, vacuum toilet systems, oil water separators, push-fit piping systems and fittings, fire and gas detection systems, and chlorination packages for naval establishments, shipyards, and industrial facilities. In line with industry requirements, Hi-Point Services (I) Pvt. Ltd. further diversified into areas including anti-fouling systems for industrial applications, marine growth prevention systems, gas metering and custody transfer solutions, industrial burners and gas train systems, signaling devices and hazardous area communication systems, high-performance protective coating solutions for abrasion, corrosion, and chemical resistance, and portable water treatment systems.

To support this expansion, Hi-Point Services (I) Pvt. Ltd. strengthened its internal engineering capabilities by developing a dedicated team responsible for detailed engineering, system integration, and analytical studies including Finite Element Analysis (FEA). These capabilities enabled the company to undertake more complex projects requiring customized engineering solutions and high reliability in demanding marine and industrial environments.

A significant milestone in the company’s growth was the establishment of its R&D, manufacturing and integration facility at Visakhapatnam, which enables Hi-Point Services (I) Pvt. Ltd. to manufacture, assemble, integrate, test, and conduct performance trials for various systems before deployment at project sites. This development enhanced the company’s ability to deliver complete solutions under one roof while maintaining quality control and project timelines.

Through continuous technological adoption, expansion of engineering capabilities, and commitment to customer support, Hi-Point Services (I) Pvt. Ltd. has built a strong reputation in the industry. The company’s growth has been driven by long-term relationships with customers, consultants, and project stakeholders, supported by consistent performance and dependable service delivery over the years.
