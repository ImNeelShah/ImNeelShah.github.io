About Us

Our Story

Hi-Point Services (I) Pvt. Ltd. has evolved into a recognized engineering solutions provider across marine, defence, petrochemical, power, and industrial sectors in India. Starting with strong roots in the marine industry, the company built its reputation through consistent project execution and reliable service support across ports, shipyards, and naval establishments—including remote locations like Port Blair.

Built on Experience. Driven by Excellence

⊚

Our Evolution

From a service-focused organization handling installation, commissioning, and maintenance, Hi-Point has evolved into a full-scale engineering solutions provider. We expanded into turnkey project execution, developed advanced system integration capabilities, and established our manufacturing & R&D facility in Visakhapatnam.

Key Milestone

End-to-end engineering solutions delivered under one roof.

⊚

Our Strengths

Hi-Point’s capabilities are built on strong in-house engineering expertise and advanced analytical capabilities, including FEA. Backed by global collaborations and a robust manufacturing and testing facility, we deliver reliable and high-performance solutions across industries.

What sets us apart

A strong pan-India service network ensuring consistent, responsive, and dependable execution.
