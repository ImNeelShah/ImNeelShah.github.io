About Us

Our Story

Hi-Point Services (I) Pvt. Ltd. has grown into a recognized engineering solutions provider across marine, defence, petrochemical, power, and industrial sectors in India.

With strong roots in the marine industry, the company built its reputation by delivering specialized equipment and reliable technical services to ports, shipyards, and naval establishments across the country—including remote locations such as Port Blair.

Through consistent project execution and dependable service support, Hi-Point steadily expanded its presence across major and minor ports in India.

Expanding Capabilities

As the organization evolved, Hi-Point diversified its expertise beyond services into turnkey project execution and system integration—covering design, engineering, supply, installation, commissioning, and lifecycle support.

With continuous technological advancement and global collaborations, the company expanded its portfolio to include sanitation systems, gas detection solutions, water treatment technologies, anti-fouling systems, industrial equipment, and high-performance protective solutions.

This evolution enabled Hi-Point to support complex engineering requirements across marine and industrial environments with reliability and precision.

Built on Experience. Driven by Excellence

Our Evolution

From a service-focused organization handling installation, commissioning, and maintenance, Hi-Point has evolved into a full-scale engineering solutions provider. We expanded into turnkey project execution, developed advanced system integration capabilities, and established our manufacturing & R&D facility in Visakhapatnam.

Key Milestone

End-to-end engineering solutions delivered under one roof.

Our Strengths

Hi-Point’s capabilities are built on strong in-house engineering expertise and advanced analytical capabilities, including FEA. Backed by global collaborations and a robust manufacturing and testing facility, we deliver reliable and high-performance solutions across industries.

What sets us apart

A strong pan-India service network ensuring consistent, responsive, and dependable execution.

Our People
